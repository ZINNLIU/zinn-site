About

刘紫茵（ZINN），艺术策展人与研究员，现任深圳市人工智能与机器人研究院（AIRS）科学+艺术联合实验室主创。

ZINN (LIU Ziyin) is an art curator and researcher, currently serving as Founding Member of SciArt Co-Lab at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for Society (AIRS), Shenzhen.

她的实践游走于科技、艺术与社会之间，关注人机交互、身体感知与未来叙事。策展之外，她亦是一位当代表演者——在舞蹈、戏剧与实验影像中探索身体与语言的边界。

Her practice navigates the intersections of science, art and society, with a focus on human-machine interaction, bodily perception, and speculative futures. Beyond curation, she is also a contemporary performer — exploring the boundaries of body and language through dance, theatre, and experimental film.
